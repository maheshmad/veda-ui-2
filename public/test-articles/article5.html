Klauer Manufacturing, EMCO Building Products, supplier news

Klauer Manufacturing Company and EMCO Building Products announce plans to form a strategic partnership that would use their complementary strengths to create an alliance, making them one of the strongest companies in the steel and aluminum building products sector.

Klauer has purchased EMCO's machinery and production capability and will consolidate all manufacturing operations to Dubuque, Iowa. EMCO will continue to market the EMCO brand of steel siding and matching metal soffit, fascia and rain carrying products throughout North America while Klauer will introduce a new line of siding products to its customers. EMCO will also market existing Klauer brand sheet metal building products.

"The acquisition of the EMCO manufacturing equipment and inventories along with the formation of the marketing alliance represents an exciting time at Klauer and we feel very fortunate to be able to continue working with the talented EMCO staff." said Mike Igo, Chief Operating Officer of Klauer Manufacturing Company. "This offers us the opportunity to further establish ourselves as one of the industry's premier sheet metal products manufacturers by offering the most comprehensive line of products available in the industry.

According to Mike Bullinger, president of EMCO Building Products, "This partnership will capitalize on EMCO's product and marketing strengths and Klauer's manufacturing and distribution strengths to improve the overall value proposition to our customers. EMCO will continue to be the leader in the steel siding arena by providing our industry with superior steel products for the marketplace and by placing a greater focus on servicing the needs of today's customer." Bullinger goes on to say that, "Currently our steel siding products offer the best value in performance, fade protection, color selection, maintenance ease, and sustainabilityâ€¦not to mention it is backed by the strongest manufacturer warranty in the industry." Additionally, this alliance will help expand EMCO's sister company Eastside Machine in their distribution of seamless portable roll-forming machines (siding, gutter & roofing) throughout the United States.

"Likewise, I am excited about this partnership with EMCO," said Mike Igo, chief operations officer of Klauer Manufacturing. "We are one of the industry's premier sheet metal products manufacturers and by adding a complete line of steel siding products to our offering is a great opportunity for us to utilize our capacity more efficiently as well as making the products available to our Klauer customers."

The manufacturing transition is expected to be complete by the end of February.

More about Klauer Manufacturing
Klauer Manufacturing has been a trusted leader in the sheet metal building industry since 1870. Located on the banks of the Mississippi River, in Dubuque, Iowa, Klauer is a family-owned business operated by fourth and fifth generation family members. From its two locations in Dubuque, Klauer Mfg. produces a wide variety of sheet metal building products servicing the residential, agricultural and commercial construction industries. Products are manufactured from steel and aluminum, both in mill and more than 150 pre-painted color finishes. From roofing, rainware and siding products to metal building components, Klauer Manufacturing provides one of the most comprehensive products offering in the industry.
